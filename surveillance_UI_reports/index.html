Departments & database maintenance

Departments

Departments available when reclassifying visitors into employees.

Add employee

Register a person now by uploading one or more clear, front-facing face photos. The uploaded photo becomes their fixed profile picture (it never changes on its own), and the system remembers their face for recognition.

Daily reset

Clear all Unknowns (people not yet confirmed as Visitors). Confirmed Visitors and Employees are kept, so returning people are still recognised. Runs automatically every midnight.

Merge duplicate visitors

The same person can enroll as several Visitor cards when their face was captured from different angles. This finds Visitors whose faces match (face only — never clothing) and folds them into one. Preview first; nothing changes until you apply.

Danger zone

Permanently delete every person, event and snapshot from the database. Cameras are kept. This cannot be undone.
